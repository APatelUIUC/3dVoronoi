Honeycomb & Voronoi

The Mathematics of Nature's Architecture

Bees construct their honeycomb cells in a hexagonal pattern — but why?

The answer lies in Voronoi tessellation: when space is divided so each region contains all points closest to a seed point, hexagonal patterns emerge naturally.
